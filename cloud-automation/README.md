# Cloud Automation

Multi-cloud automation and operations across **AWS, Azure, GCP, and Oracle Cloud** —
provisioned as code, monitored, and cost-controlled.

## What I do
- **Provisioning & lifecycle** — infrastructure stood up via IaC and cloud CLIs/APIs, not the console.
- **Hybrid connectivity** — site-to-site and WireGuard tunnels stitching on-prem to cloud.
- **Cost discipline** — right-sized / always-free footprints running real production-style services,
  with monitoring and alerting.
- Backed by 10+ cloud certifications (AWS and Azure) plus GCP.

## Where the field is (2024–2026)
Multi-cloud has shifted from risk-hedging to a deliberate architecture choice, and two themes now
dominate operations: **FinOps** and **platform engineering**. FinOps went mainstream fast — by 2026
nearly all practitioners report managing **AI/GenAI spend** as part of cloud cost (up from roughly a
third two years earlier) — and teams increasingly build **Internal Developer Platforms** that are
Kubernetes-native, GitOps-driven, and surface cost at the moment of a deployment decision. My
approach lines up with that: everything as code, observable, and accountable for what it costs to run.
